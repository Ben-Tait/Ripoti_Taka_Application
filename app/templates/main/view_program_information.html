The Ripoti Taka Program is an innovative initiative that empowers citizens to report and track waste-related issues using a web and mobile application. Our mission is to promote a cleaner environment by engaging communities and stakeholders in efficient waste management practices.

Key Features

  • Submit waste-related issue reports with geolocation and mapping.
  • Receive real-time updates on issue resolutions.
  • Track the status of submitted reports.
  • Engage in gamification and earn rewards for active participation.
  • Access data analytics for informed waste management decisions.
  • Ensure inclusivity with support for multiple languages.
  • Collaborate with local authorities for swift issue resolution.
  • Integrated educational resources for waste management awareness.

Community Engagement

We believe that community involvement is crucial for effective waste management. By providing a platform for citizens to report issues and contribute to cleaner surroundings, we aim to create a stronger sense of responsibility towards the environment.

Sustainability Goals

The Ripoti Taka Program aligns with global sustainability goals by promoting responsible waste disposal, reducing illegal dumping, and fostering a culture of environmental consciousness.
